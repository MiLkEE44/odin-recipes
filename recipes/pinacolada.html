Pina Colada Recipe

There's nothing like a Pina Colada on a Puerto Rico beach. This Caribbean flavored drink will remind you of the island no matter where you are.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of coconut milk
  • 1 cup of pineapple juice
  • 4 tablespoons of white sugar
  • 8 cubes of ice

Directions

  1. In a blender combine coconut milk, pineapple juice, sugar and ice. Blend until smooth. Then pour into a glass and its ready to be served.
